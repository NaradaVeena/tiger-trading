## Key Themes Across All 8 Stocks

1. **Valuations are universally stretched.** Every stock in this group trades at a significant premium to historical averages. The AI infrastructure theme is well-recognized by the market.

2. **Backlogs and order books are exceptional.** NVT ($2.3B), PWR ($39.2B), COHR (4x B2B), FN (pre-sold capacity) ‚Äî demand visibility is the best it's been in the data center cycle.

3. **The strongest moats are labor and manufacturing.** FN (sole 1.6T manufacturer), PWR (68K workers), COHR (InP vertical integration) ‚Äî these advantages take years to replicate.

4. **The smartest money is being patient.** The best entries come on pullbacks, not breakouts. Every stock in this list has had 10-20% drawdowns even during the AI mega-rally.

5. **Diversification across the stack reduces single-point risk.** If optical wins over copper (bad for CRDO, good for FN/COHR), or if grid buildout slows (bad for PWR, fine for NVT), a diversified basket captures the overall trend.
