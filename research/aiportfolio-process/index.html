üêØ Trading Rules & Process

Systematic rules governing the Tiger Portfolio. Every trade must comply. No exceptions.

üß† Core Philosophy

üêØ Tiger cub approach: Concentrated, high-conviction, patient. 8-12 positions max.
üîß Hardware moats only: Physical assets that take years to replicate. No pure software plays.
üêã Don't be whale exit liquidity: Buy into volume nodes, not thin air. If VP is thin below, you're the exit.
üíµ Cash is a position: No rush to deploy. Cash IS the hedge until 70%+ invested.

üìê Position Sizing

  1. Max single position: 25% of portfolio
  2. Core positions: 15-25% each (highest conviction + best chart)
  3. Starter positions: 3-5% (strong thesis, chart needs work)
  4. Watch list: $0 until chart confirms entry
  5. Scale in: Never deploy full size at once. 50% initial, add on confirmation.

üü¢ Entry Rules

  1. Volume profile check: Only buy into thick volume nodes. Thin VP below = you're someone else's exit liquidity.
  2. RSI filter: Prefer RSI < 50. Never chase RSI > 70 unless breakout on massive volume.
  3. No buying at channel tops: Upper trendline/channel resistance = wait for pullback.
  4. Breakout confirmation: Don't front-run resistance. Wait for break + retest + hold on volume.
  5. Post-earnings washout = opportunity: Beat + sell = sentiment reset, not fundamental problem.
  6. Divergence = caution: Bearish RSI divergence means reduce size. Don't ignore the signal.

üìà LEAPS Rules

  1. Only on oversold names: LEAPS on extended charts = paying theta to lose.
  2. Delta > 0.40: No far OTM lottery tickets.
  3. Minimum 18 months to expiry: Time is the asset.
  4. Max 10% of portfolio: Leverage is a tool, not a strategy.

üî¥ Exit Rules

  1. Thesis invalidation = exit: If the reason you bought breaks, sell. No hoping.
  2. Stop-loss by thesis, not price: Each position has documented invalidation triggers.
  3. Trim on extension: Position up 50%+ with RSI > 70 ‚Üí trim 20-30%.
  4. Let winners run: Don't sell just because it's green. Winners get bigger.
  5. Cut losers fast: Down 15%+ and thesis weakening ‚Üí cut. Don't average down into broken thesis.

üõ°Ô∏è Hedge Rules

  1. No hedges while cash > 30%: Cash IS the hedge. Don't pay theta on SPY puts with $70K in cash.
  2. Deploy hedges at 70%+ invested: Index puts or short book plays when most capital deployed.
  3. Short book = directional puts: No short selling stocks. Buy puts on disruption losers.
