## Core Philosophy
- **Tiger cub approach**: Concentrated, high-conviction, patient
- **Hardware moats only**: Physical assets that take years to replicate
- **Don't be whale exit liquidity**: Buy into volume nodes, not thin air
- **Cash is a position**: No rush to deploy. Cash IS the hedge until 70%+ invested.

## Position Sizing Rules
1. **Max single position**: 25% of portfolio
2. **Core positions**: 15-25% each (highest conviction, best chart setup)
3. **Starter positions**: 3-5% (thesis is strong but chart needs work)
4. **Watch list**: $0 allocated until chart confirms entry
5. **Scale in, don't lump**: Never deploy full position size at once. 50% initial, add on confirmation.

## Entry Rules
1. **Volume profile check**: Only buy into thick volume nodes. If the VP shows thin air below current price, you're liquidity for someone else's exit.
2. **RSI filter**: Prefer entries with RSI < 50. Never chase RSI > 70 unless breakout on massive volume.
3. **No buying at channel tops**: If price is at upper channel/trendline resistance, wait for pullback.
4. **Breakout confirmation**: For resistance tests (e.g., NVT $121), don't front-run. Wait for break + retest + hold on volume.
5. **Post-earnings washout = opportunity**: Beat + sell = sentiment reset, not fundamental problem. Best entries come 2-5 days after earnings overreaction.
6. **Divergence = caution**: Bearish RSI divergence (higher price, lower RSI) means reduce size. Don't ignore the signal.

## LEAPS Rules
1. **Only on oversold names**: LEAPS on extended charts = paying theta to lose
2. **Delta > 0.40**: No far OTM lottery tickets
3. **Minimum 18 months to expiry**: Time is the asset
4. **Max 10% of portfolio in LEAPS**: Leverage is a tool, not a strategy

## Exit Rules
1. **Thesis invalidation = exit**: If the reason you bought breaks, sell. No hoping.
2. **Stop-loss by thesis, not price**: Each position has a specific invalidation trigger documented in the thesis tracker.
3. **Trim on extension**: If a position runs 50%+ and RSI > 70, trim 20-30% to lock gains.
4. **Let winners run**: Don't sell just because it's green. Winners get bigger.
5. **Cut losers fast**: If a position is down 15%+ and thesis is weakening, cut it. Don't average down into a broken thesis.

## Hedge Rules
1. **No hedges while cash > 30%**: Cash IS the hedge. Don't pay theta on SPY puts while sitting on $70K cash.
2. **Deploy hedges at 70%+ invested**: Index puts or short book plays when most capital is deployed.
3. **Short book = directional puts**: No short selling stocks. Buy puts on AI disruption losers.

## Daily Workflow

### 11 AM EST ‚Äî Proforma Review
1. Check pre-market/morning action on all tracked tickers
2. Check overnight news, analyst upgrades/downgrades, sector moves
3. Review all rules above before proposing any trade
4. Propose proforma trades with full reasoning
5. Post proformas to Horsey group + update dashboard
6. **Wait for feedback before 2 PM lock**

### 2 PM EST ‚Äî Trade Lock
1. Review any feedback on proformas
2. Lock approved trades at prevailing prices (buy at ask, sell at bid)
3. Cancel/modify trades based on feedback
4. Update all position prices
5. Take daily portfolio snapshot
6. Add journal entry with market context
7. Check thesis on each position ‚Äî flag changes
8. Regenerate dashboard
9. Post summary to Horsey group

### Weekly (Friday Close)
1. Full thesis review on every position
2. Grade each position: performing / underperforming / thesis at risk
3. Plan next week's potential actions
4. Update rules if lessons learned

## Anti-Sycophancy Rules
1. **Don't validate every trade**: Just give data.
2. **If the chart says wait, say wait**: Don't find reasons to buy because the fundamentals are exciting.
3. **Acknowledge mistakes**: Wrong calls get documented, not buried.
4. **No fortune cookie wisdom**: Data and levels, not platitudes.
