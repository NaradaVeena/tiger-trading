## Executive Summary

The AI infrastructure buildout is entering its second phase ‚Äî moving from GPU compute to the connectivity, power, cooling, and physical infrastructure that enables 1M+ accelerator clusters. Below are 14 candidates across 6 layers of the stack, each profiled with financials and Tiger Portfolio fit assessment.
